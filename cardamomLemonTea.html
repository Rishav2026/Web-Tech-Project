Cardamom Lemon Tea


Cardamom Lemon Tea Ingridients: Pure Leaf Lemon Tea 2 1-inch Slices of Fresh Ginger 2 Cardamom Pods 2 Pieces of Lemon Peel Instructions: Simmer over medium-low heat for 10 minutes. Serve in a mug with a splash of milk, if desired.
